Investigating a Dataset¶

Titanic Data:¶

The Titanic dataset consist of the list of passangers who survived the incident. Out of 2224 passengers and crew members, only 891 were able to survive.

Questions related to Titanic Dataset:¶

  1. What factors made people more likely to survive?
  2. Does the surviving rate depend upon the age or sex of the people?
  3. Does Higher class people survive more when compared to lower class people?

Some Predictions that could increase Survivability:¶

There are some data which could have some impact on the Survival rate. The people who spent more for the ticket, they would have got more facilities when compared to people who spent less. The cabin to which passengers belonged can also place its impact on Survival rate since the passengers whose cabin was far from boats could take more time to reach the boats when compared to the near cabins. If the incident occured in the day time, the survival rate would have increased or the incident could have been avoided. If the hull of ship was strong enough to sustain the colloidal, then the ship could have avoided the wreak.

Conclusion:¶

In [182]:
from scipy import stats as st
st.ttest_ind(survived_people['Age'], dead_people['Age'])
Out[182]:
Ttest_indResult(statistic=-2.0666869462538102, pvalue=0.039124654013482488)

From the above analysis, we say that the passengers whose age is less than 40 are more likely to survive. But, this doesn't prove that being younger will increase the chance of Survival. Thus proving Correlation doesn't imply Causation.
The number of Female passengers survived is higher than the male passengers. From this, we can infer that the gender affects the Survival rate.
H0: μc < μi
Whereas, Ha: μc > = μi
In this Analysis, the Null Hypothesis is rejected because the t-statistics value is less than t-critical value (i.e) -1.64 < -2.06. So, the Alternative Hypothesis is selected.
The Survival is also dependent upon the Pclass. The Upper class people are more likely to survive because they are rich and get more facilities when compared to the lower class people.

The disadvantage of this dataset is that there are several missing values in the table. The missing values affect the Analysis of the Data. Since the data is missing, the computations done above cannot be said accurate.
